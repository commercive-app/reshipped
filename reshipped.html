The process

From returned to reshipped — in four steps.

01
Your customer ships back
We provide pre-paid return labels through your store. The package heads straight to our fulfillment centers.
02
We inspect & grade
Every item is photographed, condition-graded, and logged to your Reshipped dashboard within 24 hours of arrival.
03
Inventory auto-syncs
Grade-A items are relisted to your store automatically. Grade-B items are discounted. Grade-C goes to liquidation partners.
04
New order, reshipped
When the next customer orders, we ship from returned stock in your branded packaging — indistinguishable from new.

Why Reshipped

Returns are revenue waiting to happen.

Most brands treat returns as a cost center. We turn them into a second sale. Every return that flows through Reshipped is inspected, restocked, and on its way to a new customer — often within 72 hours.
